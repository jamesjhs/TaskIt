Inside a task's detail panel, scroll down to find the Progress Notes section. Click + Add Note, type your update, and save. Notes are visible to everyone in the task's group.

8. Deleting Your Account (Right to Erasure)

Under UK GDPR you have the right to have your personal data erased. Jobber lets you do this yourself, instantly, without contacting the administrator.

⚠️ Delete My Account

Permanently delete your account and all associated data (tasks, notes, group memberships). This cannot be undone.

Delete My Account
1
Go to 👤 Profile → scroll to the bottom → ⚠️ Delete My Account.
2
Confirm twice when prompted. This is intentionally a two-step process to prevent accidental deletion.
3
All your data — tasks, notes, group memberships, and your account — are permanently and immediately removed.
ℹ️ If you were the sole admin of a group, that group and all its tasks are also deleted. If there are other group admins, the group continues without you.
⚠️ User reports you submitted or that were made against you may be retained for up to 7 years under safeguarding obligations. See the Privacy Policy for details.

9. 🎮 Gamification — XP, Achievements, Streaks & Freeze Credits

Jobber's optional gamification system rewards consistent task completion. Enable it from your Profile at any time — your progress is preserved if you toggle it off and back on.

ℹ️ Gamification is fully opt-in and never affects the core task management experience for users who prefer it off.

9.1 Enabling Gamification

1
Go to 👤 Profile → find the Gamification card.
2
Click Enable Gamification. The card expands to show your XP, skills, achievements, and streaks.

9.5 ❄️ Freeze Credits

Earn 1 Freeze Credit for every task you complete. Spend 1 credit to apply a Freeze to any recurring task — this protects the streak against one missed deadline.

1
Open the task detail panel for a recurring task.
2
Click Apply Freeze. Your freeze credit balance decreases by 1 and a ❄️ icon appears on the task.
3
If the task becomes overdue, the Freeze absorbs the miss — the streak is preserved and the ❄️ is cleared automatically by the hourly scheduler.
💡 Tip: Each task can hold one active Freeze at a time. Apply a Freeze before you know you'll be busy — you can't retroactively protect a streak that's already been reset.
