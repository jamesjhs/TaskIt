How TaskIt! works

Getting started takes seconds. No complex setup, no lengthy onboarding — create your account, add a task, and start getting things done.

1

Create your account

Sign up in seconds with just your email and a password. Verify your email and you're ready to go.

2

Add your first task

Give it a title, pick a type, set a due date, and add notes. Keep it as simple or as detailed as you need.

3

Invite your team

Create a group and share the invite link or QR code. Collaborators join instantly and can see tasks right away.

4

Never miss a deadline

Receive email and popup reminders before tasks are due. Subscribe to your personal ICS calendar feed and let your calendar app do the rest.

5

Level up as you go

Enable gamification from your Profile. Earn XP for every completed task, level up, unlock achievements, and build daily streaks that keep motivation high.

🤝 Friends

What are TaskIt! Friends?
Friends let you compete on XP leaderboards with people outside your groups — colleagues, housemates, anyone you know. You're never added to each other's groups; you just see each other's total XP rank. There are three ways to connect:
  • Share your invite link or QR code — valid for 7 days, single-use.
  • Share your username + friend key — your friend enters them directly in the app.
  • Both of you must be registered — if the other person is new to TaskIt!, send them the invite link to register first, then connect.
