How Crystallise works

Getting started is quick and easy. No complicated setup, no lengthy onboarding — just create, assign, and get things done.

1

Create your account

Sign up in seconds with just your email and a password. Verify your email and you're ready to go.

2

Add your first task

Give it a title, pick a type, set a due date, and add notes. Keep it as simple or as detailed as you need.

3

Invite your team

Create a group and share the invite link or QR code. Collaborators join instantly and can see tasks straight away.

4

Stay on top of deadlines

Receive email and popup reminders before tasks are due. Integrate with your calendar app so nothing slips through.

🤝 Friends

What are Crystallise Friends?
Friends let you compete on XP leaderboards with people outside your groups — colleagues, housemates, anyone you know. You're never added to each other's groups; you just see each other's total XP rank. There are three ways to connect:
  • Share your invite link or QR code — valid for 7 days, single-use.
  • Share your username + friend key — your friend enters them directly in the app.
  • Both of you must be registered — if the other person is new to Crystallise, send them the invite link to register first, then connect.
