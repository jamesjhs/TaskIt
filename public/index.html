Jobber is a fast, lightweight collaborative task manager — create tasks, set due dates, organise by category, and keep your team in sync, all from your phone or browser. No ads, no clutter.

📅 Calendar Integration

Subscribe to your Jobber tasks in any calendar app (Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook, etc.) using the private link below. Keep this link secret — anyone with it can view your tasks.

💬 Feedback & Feature Requests

Have a suggestion or spotted a bug? Let us know. Tick the box if you'd like admin to reply to you in-app.

⚠️ Do not include personal or sensitive information (e.g. health data, financial details, government records, or trade secrets) in your feedback.

⚠️ Delete My Account

Permanently delete your account and all associated data (tasks, notes, group memberships). This cannot be undone. No administrator action is required.
